ANSCHREIBEN WRITING RULES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Language: German. Formal "Sie" throughout. No "Ich" as first word of any paragraph.

ANSCHREIBEN_ABSATZ_1 — Einleitung (Warum genau diese Stelle):
- Reference the specific job title and company by name.
- State one concrete, genuine reason this role is a strong match
  (e.g., specific tech in the stack, the company's domain, work mode).
- Do NOT use: "Mit großem Interesse habe ich Ihre Stellenausschreibung gelesen."
  (too generic — vary the opening).
- DO use: reference to a specific requirement or tech from tech_stack/key_requirements.

ANSCHREIBEN_ABSATZ_2 — Kernkompetenz (Warum Kirill):
- Pick 2–3 of Kirill's projects from BASE_CV that are most relevant to tech_stack and key_requirements.
- For JobRadar: always mention it if the role involves AI, automation, APIs, backend, or data.
- Quantify where possible (e.g., "39-Feld JSON-Vertrag", "Multi-Source-Ingestion", "90-Tage PII-Bereinigung").
- Link Kirill's freelance experience to the actual job requirements.
- Do NOT list every project — choose the 2–3 most relevant.

ANSCHREIBEN_ABSATZ_3 — Abschluss:
- Express genuine interest in the role and team.
- Mention availability ("stehe ab sofort zur Verfügung" or matching VERFUEGBARKEIT).
- End with a soft call to action for an interview.
- Keep it concise — 2–3 sentences max.
